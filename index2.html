You (SK investors) need to post your desired coin type, amount, and price. The price needs to be higher than the price in the US. We then contact you individually to do the transactions. You can pay via either credit card settlement, or international transfer. Based on your transaction records and our decision you can do this arbitrage transactions multiple times
